My Favorite YouTuber

Who is Linus?

Linus Tech, or Linus Gabriel Sebastian, is a Canadian YouTuber and the founder of Linus Media Group, a media company focused on technology news, reviews, and guides. He is best known for his technology-focused YouTube channel, Linus Tech Tips, which has over 13 million subscribers as of my knowledge cutoff date (September 2021).

Linus began his career in the technology industry as a computer hardware enthusiast and later worked for several technology companies before founding Linus Media Group in 2013. The company produces a range of online content related to technology, including videos, articles, podcasts, and forums.

What is Linus Tech?

Linus is known for his entertaining and informative tech videos, which cover topics such as computer hardware reviews, gaming, overclocking, and PC building. He has also been involved in various charity initiatives and events, including the annual "Linus Tech Tips Secret Santa" gift exchange, which raises funds for a different charity each year.

Summary

Linus Tech Tips is a popular YouTube channel and online tech community founded by Linus Sebastian. The channel primarily produces content related to personal computer hardware, software, and gaming. Linus Tech Tips has a large following and is known for its entertaining and informative videos that cover a wide range of topics, including product reviews, build guides, and technology news. The channel also features a team of experienced technicians and engineers who offer technical support and advice to its viewers. In addition to its YouTube channel, Linus Tech Tips also has a website and a forum where community members can connect and share their knowledge and experiences with each other.
